About

Carson Lundell is a professional golfer from Alpine, Utah competing on the Korn Ferry Tour. A former BYU standout, Carson was one of the most decorated collegiate golfers in program history — finishing his career with a 70.76 scoring average, 7 top-10 finishes his senior season, and individual medalist honors at the NCAA West Regional. He brings the same competitive precision to his lessons that took him from Lone Peak High School to the professional tour.
