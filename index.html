FAQ
Is there a booking fee on top of my plan?
Basic and Pro plans include a 5% booking fee per session. Elite members pay zero booking fees — keeping 100% of every session rate.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no contracts. Cancel anytime from your dashboard.
How do I get paid?
Payments are processed securely via Stripe and deposited directly to your bank account within 2 business days of each session.
Do students pay to use Cadi?
No. Cadi is completely free for golfers to browse, book, and review instructors.
What if I'm just starting out?
Basic is a great starting point. You can upgrade to Pro or Elite at any time as your bookings grow.
